Function Decomposition and Approximation

A summary of talk with Prof.Shen November 10, 2016

There are basically four components of a function: global pattern, local structure, global structure, random parts.

  • Global Pattern is the recurvise pattern of the function, and could be approximated by periodic function.
  • Local Structure is the local feature of a function on a small scale, and could be approcimated by wavelets.
  • Global Structure is the feature on a large scale, for example, the whole shape of a house. This could be related to people's recognition, and deep learning now might take an effect.
  • Random part is often the noisy component of the function.

    • For other more practical function decomposition, when we decompose a function, we should take care of the meaning in the reality of each obtained component, that is, to understand what this subfunction represents for.
