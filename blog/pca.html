PCA: Principle Component Analysis

Goal $X \in R^{m*n}$, with $n$ samples and $m$ features. Hope to find $p$(no greater than $m$) so that these directions are where data has largest variance.

Method, SVD

  • Centralize data for each direction. $ X->X1 $;

  • $ X1=WDV'; Y=W'X=DV' $

    • Comment

  • PCA for dimension reduction: discard the back rows of $Y$

  • Geometric Meaning of SVD: X1 can be seen as a linear transformation matrix. $X1(Vi)=di*Ui$
